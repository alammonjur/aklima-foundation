Our Mission

Our mission is "Education for All." We work to remove practical barriers that keep families from thriving—school fees, lack of assistive devices, unsafe roads, and preventable illness. We fund local solutions, measure outcomes, and report transparently so every rupee creates meaningful change.

Where we work

Initial focus in Magrahat (South 24 Parganas), West Bengal, with plans to expand through partnerships with local panchayats, schools, and health workers.

📍 Magrahat • Diamond Harbour • Kakdwip

Focus Areas

Six priorities guide our grants and programs.

🎓

Education Access

Scholarships, exam fees, uniforms, and mentoring for students from low‑income families.

♿

Disability Support

Wheelchairs, crutches, tricycles/bicycles, and assistive aids with after‑care.

🚧

Safe Infrastructure

Community‑led road repair, lighting, and safe‑routes‑to‑school projects.

🩺

Primary Healthcare

Screenings, health camps, and referral support for chronic conditions.

👩‍👩‍👧

Women & Livelihoods

🌱
